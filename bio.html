About Siming Chen

Siming Chen is a junior at Tufts University who studies ILVS (International Letter & Visual Studies) and French, with a minor in Computer Science. She believes in the innovative and everlasting power of art and she craves to find the intersection between art and science. With a major in ILVS, she is able to gain knowledge in art history and media theory that deepens her comprehension of relation between art and daily life. Meanwhile, she loves different cultures and she enjoys the intercultural exchange realized by sharing the language. She can speak Chinese, English and French fluently and she is now working hard to make progress in her Japanese language study.
